## v2 Changes (2026-05-01)

### UI
- **Font sizes bumped ~15-20%** across all slide types
- **Image support added**: `layout: 'columns'` puts image next to text; otherwise image renders above text block
- **Max-width enlarged** from 720px → 800px (860px on large screens)
- **Larger bullet dots** (10px) and increased line-height for readability
- **`<b>` markup support** — content strings can contain `<b>text</b>` for emphasis

- **Full structure**: 25 slides total, more compact but richer per-slide

### Key Design Choices for Engagement
1. **Opens with a quiz** → grabs attention, creates curiosity
2. **Answers to quiz revealed mid-talk** → rewards those who paid attention
3. **German cultural "inside baseball"** throughout → authentic, "can't Google this" appeal
4. **Practical takeaways for Chinese business audience** → directly relevant
5. **"权力转移" framing** throughout → translates technical changes into business language
